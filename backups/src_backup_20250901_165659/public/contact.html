Frequently Asked Questions
  • What is SHODESH?
    SHODESH is a web-based donation management platform connecting donors, staff, foundations, and individuals in need to create a transparent, trackable, and trustworthy donation ecosystem in Bangladesh.
  • How do I donate to a campaign?
    You can donate by clicking the "Donate Now" button on the homepage or by browsing and selecting a campaign from the Categories or Search pages.
  • Is my donation secure?
    Yes, all donations are processed through secure payment gateways and are tracked for transparency.
  • How can I track my donation?
    After donating, you will receive a confirmation and can track your donation status through your account dashboard.
  • Who can start a fundraising campaign?
    Anyone can start a campaign by signing up and submitting their cause for review and approval by the SHODESH team.
  • How do I join the SHODESH team as a volunteer?
    Click on the "Join Our Team" link in the header and fill out the volunteer application form.
  • Can I donate anonymously?
    Yes, you can choose to hide your name when making a donation.
  • How does SHODESH ensure transparency?
    Every donation is tracked and verified, and regular updates are provided to donors and beneficiaries.
  • What types of causes can I support?
    You can support health, education, livelihood, animal welfare, and many other causes across Bangladesh.
  • How do I contact SHODESH for support?
    You can reach us through this Contact page, email us at contact@shodesh.org, or call +880 1712 345 678.
  • Are there any fees for using SHODESH?
    SHODESH does not charge donors any fees. A small processing fee may be deducted by payment gateways.
  • How do I report a suspicious campaign?
    If you notice any suspicious activity, please report it immediately using the "Report" button on the campaign page or contact us directly.
  • Can I edit or delete my campaign after publishing?
    Yes, you can edit your campaign details or request deletion by contacting our support team.
  • How do I receive funds if I am a beneficiary?
    Once your campaign is approved and donations are collected, funds will be transferred to your verified bank account or mobile wallet.
  • What should I do if I forgot my password?
    Use the "Forgot Password" link on the Sign In page to reset your password.
