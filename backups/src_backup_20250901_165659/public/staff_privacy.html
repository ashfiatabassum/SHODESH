1. Scope

This policy applies to personal data processed about individuals acting in a staff or volunteer capacity on the SHODESH platform.

2. Data We Collect

  • Identity: First/last name, national ID (NID), date of birth.
  • Contact: Phone number, email, address (house, road, area, district, division, zip).
  • Account: Username, hashed password (planned), role/status.
  • Documentation: CV/resume (file metadata & content).
  • Operational: Activity timestamps, status changes, approval records.
  • Technical: Login timestamps, basic device/browser headers (for security logs).

3. How We Use Data

  • Identity & verification (eligibility and fraud prevention).
  • Assignment and coordination of tasks / cases.
  • Security monitoring and access management.
  • Compliance with legal or regulatory obligations.
  • Aggregated analytics to improve platform performance (non-identifying).

4. Legal Bases

We rely on one or more of the following: consent (where explicitly requested), legitimate interest in platform security and coordination, and compliance with applicable law.

5. Data Sharing

  • Internal authorized administrators and coordinators.
  • Service providers (hosting, email, security) under confidentiality and data processing terms.
  • Regulators or authorities when legally required.

6. Security Measures

  • Access control (role-based permissions, session management).
  • Audit/event logging for sensitive operations.
  • Encrypted transport (HTTPS) and planned encryption at rest for sensitive fields.
  • File type and size validation for uploads.

7. Retention

Data is retained for the active participation period plus a defined archival period (e.g., 2–5 years) or as required by law; afterwards it is anonymized or securely deleted.

8. Your Rights

Subject to local laws you may request: access, correction, deletion, restriction, or objection to certain processing. Contact us using the channel below.

9. International Transfers

If data is transferred across borders, we implement safeguards (standard contractual clauses or equivalent legal mechanisms) where required.

10. Cookies & Logs

The staff dashboard may use strictly necessary session cookies and minimal analytics. No behavioral advertising cookies are deployed.

11. Incident / Breach Response

We will investigate suspected breaches promptly and, if required by law, notify affected individuals and/or authorities within applicable timeframes.

12. Children

The platform is not intended for persons under the minimum volunteer age threshold. We do not knowingly collect data from ineligible minors.

13. Updates

We may revise this policy. Material changes will be communicated via email or dashboard notice with a new effective date.

14. Contact

Questions or requests: privacy@shodesh.local (replace with operational address).
