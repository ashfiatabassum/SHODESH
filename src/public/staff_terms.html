1. Eligibility & Acceptance

You confirm you are of lawful working/volunteering age (minimum 16 unless local law requires higher), capable of entering an agreement, and that all information supplied (identity, contact, qualifications) is accurate and current.

2. Nature of Role

Unless expressly stated in a separate written contract, your participation is non-employment in nature (volunteer / part-time staff). No guarantee of compensation, benefits, or continued assignment is provided.

3. Onboarding & Verification

  • Completion of required training modules before handling beneficiary or donor data.
  • Submission to identity and (if applicable) background checks.
  • Provision of valid national ID (NID) and any required certifications.

4. Code of Conduct

  • No harassment, discrimination, intimidation, or abusive behavior.
  • Maintain respectful, culturally sensitive communication.
  • Follow instructions from authorized coordinators / administrators.
  • Report misconduct immediately.

5. Confidentiality & Data Handling

You will treat all non-public data (beneficiary info, donor details, internal metrics, system access credentials) as confidential. You may only access data strictly necessary for assigned tasks. Confidentiality survives termination.

6. Intellectual Property

Materials, reports, media, code, or documentation you create for SHODESH (“Work Product”) become the property of the organization upon creation, unless otherwise licensed in writing.

7. Platform & System Use

  • Use only authorized accounts; do not share credentials.
  • No personal commercial use, malware distribution, or bypassing security controls.
  • Access logs and monitoring may be used to ensure compliance.

8. Security Obligations

  • Enable reasonable device security (lock screens, updated OS).
  • Report suspected breaches, lost devices, or credential compromise within 24 hours.
  • No copying sensitive data to unapproved external media or personal cloud storage.

9. Conflicts of Interest

You must promptly disclose relationships or financial interests that could improperly influence decisions (e.g., allocation of aid, vendor selection).

10. Prohibited Activities

  • Misrepresenting authority or forging documents.
  • Soliciting funds without written authorization.
  • Using beneficiary or donor data for personal purposes.
  • Manipulating records, statistics, or eligibility data.

11. Health, Safety & Incident Reporting

All safety incidents, suspected abuse, fraud, exploitation, or protection risks must be reported immediately through designated channels.

12. Records & Documentation

You will maintain accurate, timely, and honest records. Alteration or deletion of official data to conceal errors or misconduct is forbidden.

13. Suspension & Termination

We may suspend or terminate access at any time for policy breaches, security risks, inactivity, or organizational need. Upon termination you must cease all representation and return/delete confidential materials.

14. Dispute Handling

Concerns should first be escalated internally. If unresolved, applicable local laws (jurisdiction specified by the organization) will govern.

15. Modifications

We may update these Terms. Material changes will be communicated; continued use after the effective date constitutes acceptance.

16. Notices & Communication

Official notices may be sent via the platform dashboard or your registered email. Keep your email current.

17. Acknowledgment

By checking the agreement box on the signup form you acknowledge you have read, understood, and agree to these Terms.
