About Shodesh

Shodesh is a web-based donation management platform that connects donors, staff, foundations, and individuals in need to create a transparent, trackable, and trustworthy donation ecosystem in Bangladesh.

Our mission is to bridge the gap between those who want to help and those who need help, ensuring that every donation reaches its intended destination and makes a real impact in people's lives.

Transparency

Every donation is tracked and verified to ensure complete transparency

Impact

Direct connection between donors and beneficiaries for maximum impact

Innovation

Using technology to revolutionize charitable giving in Bangladesh
