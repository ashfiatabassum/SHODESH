What to Do in an Emergency

  • Stay as calm as possible.
  • Dial 999 for immediate assistance or call one of the dedicated ambulance numbers listed above.
  • Clearly state your location, the nature of the emergency, and the number of people involved.
  • Follow any instructions given by the operator until help arrives.
