About Shodesh

Shodesh is a web-based donation management platform that connects donors, staff, foundations, and individuals in need to create a transparent, trackable, and trustworthy donation ecosystem in Bangladesh.

Our mission is to bridge the gap between those who want to help and those who need help, ensuring that every donation reaches its intended destination and makes a real impact in people's lives.

Transparency

Every donation is tracked and verified to ensure complete transparency

Impact

Direct connection between donors and beneficiaries for maximum impact

Innovation

Using technology to revolutionize charitable giving in Bangladesh

Your Role as a Staff

As a staff with Shodesh, you'll play a crucial role in transforming lives and communities across Bangladesh. Here's what you can expect:

Field Verification

Visit beneficiaries to verify their needs and ensure donations reach the right people

Data Collection

Gather and update information about individuals and families in need

Distribution Support

Help organize and distribute donations to beneficiaries in your area

Community Outreach

Spread awareness about Shodesh and help connect more people to our platform

Impact Monitoring

Track and report the impact of donations to ensure accountability

Communication Bridge

Serve as a liaison between donors, beneficiaries, and the Shodesh team

What You'll Gain

  • Make a direct positive impact in your community
  • Gain valuable experience in social work and community development
  • Build a network of like-minded individuals
  • Receive certificates and recognition for your contributions
  • Develop skills in project management and communication
  • Be part of a movement that's changing Bangladesh
